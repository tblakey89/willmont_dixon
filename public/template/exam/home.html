Introduction

Welcome to Willmott Dixon's Pre-Enrolment.

Completing this online register provides you with the safety knowledge neccessary for you to operate on sites.

You will need your CSCS card and a headshot photograph you can upload.

Registering on this system and completing the online test every 12 months will allow you to work on all our sites.

This system replaces the health and safety induction you would normally sit when arriving onto every new site. This means you can now get to work after just a short site specific induction.
