Welcome to Willmott Dixon's Pre-Enrolment

Completing this online register provides you with the health, safety and environmental knowledge necessary for you to operate on sites.

You will need your CSCS card and a headshot photograph you can upload.

Registering on this system and completing the online test every 12 months will allow you to work on all our sites.

This system replaces the health, safety and environmental briefing you would normally sit when arriving onto every new site. This means you can now get to work after just a short site specific briefing.
